Fun Facts:


Where Garfield Got His Name

Jim Davis, the creator of Garfield, wanted to follow the tradition started by other cartoonists
of naming a character after a real person. He decided to name his main character after his
grandfather, James A. Garfield Davis... who was named after the 20th President of the United
States, James A. Garfield.


How Old is Garfield (the Comic)

The first comic strip was in 1978, making Garfield 45 as of 2023.
